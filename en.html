Studio Ghibli is an animation studio located in Japan. It was founded in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata. Studio Ghibli is most recognized for their movie My neighbor Totoro. They animate all by hand which is an interesting thing since it is still such good quality movies. Their movies have precision to details and the stories are built up innovative, fascinating and emotionally touching. Studio Ghibli has won several awards for their work, one of them being Academy Awards for Best Animated Feature.

Movies

Ponyo is a movie about a son of a sailor; a 5-year old boy named Sosuke. He lives his life quite by the oceanside, mostly with his mother. One day, Sosuke finds an adorable goldfish stuck in a glass bottle. He breaks the bottle to break out the fish but then the ocean behaves differently. The goldfish is Ponyo, daughter of a masterful wizard and a sea goddess. Ponyo uses her fathers magic to transform herself into a human. She quickly falls in love with Sosuke and life on land, and so does Sosuke. In this movie you will follow Ponyo, Sosuke and all the magic that can turn a village upside down. Watching Ponyo is an emotional rollercoaster. Happiness, sadness, fear, empathy. Though it is still a great movie for families, cozy weekends or partners. Ponyo was released in 2009

Two excited sisters, Satsuki and Mei, just moved with their father to a new house in the countryside of 1950s Japan so they could reunite with their unwell mother. The sisters now have all the time in the world for adventures and to explore their new home by the dense forest filled with mythical creatures. One morning Satsuki stumbles upon Totoro, a fluffy, enormous mythical creature with large eyes. My neighbor Totoro , 1988, is a cozy movie where you get to follow Satsuki and Mei on their adventures, exploring the new area and mysterious creatures. This movie is more suitable for children since it is an easy plot to understand and get along with.

Spirited Away, released in 2001, is about Chihiro and her family. During a long car ride they stop to find an interesting tunnel. Though they didn’t know the disturbing world behind it. When her parents undergo a mysterious transformation, Chihiro gets stuck in that world and has to protect herself from the strange spirits who seek to keep her there. Spirited Away is an enthralling movie that is hard to resist, always something new happening with an amazing plot. It is believed that this story is based on a real-life incident that Hayao Miyazaki experienced once.

During the last descending days of World War ||, the siblings Seita and Setsuko separated from their parents due to American firebombing. Now they have to survive by themselves. On their journey, they look for a safe place to live in. Seita and Setsuko must rely on one another to survive. When food and medicine start to run out, Seita must help his sister so she can survive. Grave of the Fireflies was released in 1988 and it is an awfully sad movie. It is difficult to finish watching the movie without tears falling. Still, it shows a clear input on how people actually had to live for survival.
